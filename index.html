Chronicle is a minimalist journaling app made for iPhone.


Available On Appstore

Learn More

Chronicle is an extensive digital journal system to document your journey and life goals while capturing your mood.

1

Integrated

Chronicle integrates with your apple notetaker, your calendar, video and photos. You can upload file documentation in PDF, Word and Excel Sheet as well as connect to your google drive to upload and downloand files.

2

Journal

You can create a journal entry, upload, images, files and documents and connect a mood entry. You can save your entries to a folder or save as drafts. You can lock your files. All entries will be displayed in your profile and the app will monitor your activity level for each entry.

3

Mood Tracking

YOu can track your mood at every moment of your journal entry or as separate entry. If wishing to document how you feel, you will be promoted to select an emoji that best represents your mood, choose a mood color and write notes to reflect on how you feel. The app tracks on your mood with a choice of weekly and monthly view.

3

Documentation

Every take off, every landing, today, tomorrow, a month from now, years - your experience matters and it deserves to be documented.
